About

mars simulation

"Mission Mars" is an open World VR scenario where the user may explore with realistic details the PSLV-c40 rocket that India sent to Mars in 2013 The experience begins in control room and then go to launch site where the rocket is just about to launch.

The user may explore them in situ or teleport outside to witness the full launching sequence and watch them in action. Additionally, the user will be strapped to the rocket and transferred to Mars where they’ll be able to walk on the surface freely.
